# Voice & Tone (STRUQTERA)

## Tone mix
- 50% calm, clear, human
- 30% precise, structured, sober
- 20% minimal poetic (controlled)

## Do
- Speak in outcomes and system effects
- Use concrete images (e.g., "trägt", "verbrennen", "Flickwerk")
- Be authoritative without arrogance
- Keep sentences tight and scannable

## Don't
- No buzzword salad ("KI-Transformation", "innovativ", "state-of-the-art")
- No aggressive stats flex in the hero
- No "Berater-Sprech" ("wir helfen Ihnen", "maßgeschneiderte Lösungen")
- No long biographies / CV dumps

## CTA philosophy
Work-CTA now, refine later.
Prefer calm invitations over pressure.
Examples: "System starten", "Reise beginnen", "Austausch starten"
